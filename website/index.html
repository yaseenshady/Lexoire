WHY LEXOIRE EXISTS

Voice and AI coding tools don't talk to each other.

PAIN Developers jump between Claude, Codex, and Copilot with no shared voice layer or session memory.

LOSS Spoken instructions disappear unless attached to the right session, repo, provider, and task history.

RACE CLI copilots stream, queue, fail, or wait silently — voice workflows break at every gap.

FIX Lexoire gives voice a command surface for routing, tracking, and resuming AI work across every tool.

HOW LEXOIRE WORKS

Capture. Route. Process. Persist.

01 Voice is captured and locked into an explicit session before any work begins.

02 The session context — objective, provider, history — travels with every request.

03 Claude, Codex, or Copilot processes the prompt with full shared context.

04 The response streams back visually and is spoken aloud with natural TTS.

05 Context persists for the next turn, the next provider, or the next session.
